Warranty

Contact Customer Support if you require more details about warranty policy before taking any action that may invalidate the warranty.

This warranty includes the replacement of damaged or faulty components in the terms and period described in the ordering information.

  1. A product that is subjected to abnormal use or conditions, accident, mishandling, neglect, unauthorized alteration, misuse, improper installation or repair or improper storage.
  2. A product whose serial number has been removed, altered or defaced.
