Document Conventions

Binary numbers are prefixed with a “0b”; for example, “0b0100”.

Hexadecimal numbers are prefixed with an “0x”; for example, “0x05FF”

Term Meaning
Click as in, ‘click the Add button’ Place the mouse pointer on the specified item, then press and release the left mouse button once.
Click as in, ‘click the Display\Decode\... menu’ Click the menu name, then the sub-menu name, then the sub-submenu name, etc., using the left mouse button.
Double-click as in, ‘double-click the control box’ Place the mouse pointer on the specified item, then quickly press and release the left mouse button twice.
Right-click as in, ‘right-click the drive in the topology diagram’ Place the mouse pointer on the specified item, then press and release the right mouse button once.
Close window Click on the Windows “x” symbol at the top right of a window in order to close it.
