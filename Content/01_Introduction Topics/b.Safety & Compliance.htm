Safety & Compliance

To ensure correct and safe operation, you must follow all safety precautions and instructions referred to in this user guide and in the other documentation supplied with your Integris System.

• Use the Proper Power Cord. Use only the power cord specified for use with this product and certified for the country of use.

• Check Voltage and Power. Before applying power ensure the power rating of the socket you plug the power cord into is compatible with the power rating on the label of the product.

• Ground the Product. The product is grounded through the ground conductor in the power cord. To avoid electric shock the ground conductor must be connected to earth ground.

• Power Disconnect. The power cord disconnects the product from the power source. Do not block the power cord; it must remain accessible to the user at all times.

• Use the Proper Fuse. Use only the fuse type and rating specified for this product.

• Observe all Connector Ratings. To avoid fire or shock hazard, observe all ratings and markings on the product. Consult the user guide for further ratings information before making connections to the product.

• Protect Your Eyes. Do not look directly into the transmitters of SFP’s that contain lasers.

• Do Not Operate in Wet Conditions

• Do Not Operate in an Explosive Atmosphere.

• Keep Product Surfaces Clean and Dry.

• Provide Proper Ventilation. Do not block or restrict airflow around the product. Ensure there is a minimum clearance of 2 inches (5cm) around any air intake or outlet.

• Do Not Operate with Covers or Panels Removed.

• Avoid Exposed Circuitry. Do not touch exposed connections and components when power is present.

• Do Not Operate with Suspected Failures. If you suspect there is damage to this product, have it inspected by qualified service personnel before use.

• Obey Safety Information.

Note: Integris systems are supplied as a turnkey solution consisting of the portable chassis, Interface Cards, Software and Product Licenses so no access to the internal components of the platform is necessary.
