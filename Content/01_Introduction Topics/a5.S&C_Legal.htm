About this Guide

About this Guide

This guide describes the safety and conformance information for the Integris Platform. It sets out our committment to the safe operation of our test systems, and identifyies where operating hazards could occur.

Additionally it identifies the relevant standards this product conforms to.

Throughout these guides you will see the following Note, Information and Caution boxes. They perform the following functions.

Note: Notes emphasize procedural information that may be essential to the described process

 

Information: Information boxes provide additional knowledge that might be helpful during the described process

 

Caution: Indicates a potentially hazardous situation that, if not avoided, could result in minor injury or equipment damage
