Setting Waterfall Graph Parameters

The Waterfall graph type gives a visual representation of frequency in the X axis over time in the Y axis. It is useful for seeing small changes in signal amplitude as well as picking out intermittent interferers.

Waterfall Graph

Above the top left of the waterfall graph is a scale indicating the power values related to each color on the waterfall. (See Fig 1.2 )

Color chart and Refresh setting drop-down

To the right of the color chart is the setting for the period between lines being added to the top of the waterfall. Reducing the period has the effect of speeding the waterfall up and can be useful when monitoring for short period interferers.

When you hover the mouse over the waterfall the frequency at the current location is shown at the top right of the screen. This gives a quick frequency reading for the current location and is again useful for identifying short period or intermittent interferers. (See Fig 1.3 )
