Using the Crosshairs

Menu Location  
Classic Menu GRAPH > Crosshair  
Modern Menu GRAPH > Crosshair  

Selecting Crosshair while displaying the Spectrum graph displays a set of vertical and horizontal lines centered on the current mouse position.

The crosshair values for frequency and power are displayed at the top right of the screen.

Crosshairs showing frequency and power at the current mouse position

To display crosshairs:

  1. Open the Graph menu.
  2. Select Crosshair. The crosshairs will appear on the screen at the mouse position.
