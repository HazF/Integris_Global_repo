Connecting Integris Expert

Integris Expert is connected into the CPRI link using a tap connection.

Detach the cable from the Base Band Unit (BBU) and plug it into tap port A. Now take another duplex cable and connect it between the BBU and tap port B. Next, take a third duplex cable and connect it between the tap's AB port and the receive (RX) connectors of Integris ports 0 and 1. (See Fig 1.1 )

Connecting Integris into the CPRI link

Connect the tap ports to the receive (RX) ports on ports 0 and 1 of the Integris system (See Fig 1.2 ). This may require you to separate the two connectors at the Integris end of the duplex cable.
