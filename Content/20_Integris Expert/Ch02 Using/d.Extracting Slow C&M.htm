Extracting Slow C&M Data

To capture and display Slow C&M data from the attached link

  1. Click on the C&M Capture tab
  2. Click on the CPRI Link Speed dropdown menu and select the required link speed
  3. Click on the CPRI Channel dropdown menu and select Slow C&M (See Fig 1.1 )
  4. Click on the Capture button at the bottom of the screen.

    Selecting the Slow C&M channel

  5. After a few seconds the Wireshark application will open and start to display the embedded Ethernet traffic (See Fig 1.2 )

Wireshark displaying the Slow C&M channel
