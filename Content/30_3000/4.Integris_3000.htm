Integris 3000

This section explains how to set up the Integris 3000 and connect it to the network. It shows the location and use of the connections along with details of how to start and use the system.

Integris 3000 is a 'digital' spectrum analyzer that displays power spectrum, persistence and/or waterfall graphs derived from the IQ data carried over a CPRI link.

Additional functions include RF capture and export, real-time calculation of Error Vector Magnitude (EVM), Signal to Noise Ratio (SNR); and remote control for interference hunting and collaboration.
